Meet the Characters

The Family

Meet Riley and her family members!

Mom

Riley's mom is a smart, kind woman who understands that teens and adults alike have complex emotions. She herself often feels confused how to be a good mother, and concerned about RIley's wellbeing.

Dad

Riley's dad is goofy guy who loves his family. He and Riley have a special relationship where they can have fun and play hockey. He sometimes has trouble knowing what his wife is thinking, but she loves him all the same.

Riley

Riley is a thoughtful and emotional young girl discovering her inner world.

The Emotions

Meet the emotions that guide Riley.

Joy

Joy is the exuberant and positive emotion that lights up Riley's world. She thrives on happiness and sees the best in every situation.

Sadness

Sadness is the emotion that helps Riley process difficult situations. She brings a sense of reflection and emotional depth when things feel tough.

Fear

Fear is always on the lookout for potential dangers, keeping Riley safe by alerting her to threats and helping her avoid harm.

Anger

Anger is the fiery emotion that helps Riley stand up for herself. While intense, it drives her to take action when something feels unfair or wrong.

Disgust

Disgust protects Riley from things that could harm her, whether it's food that doesn't taste right or situations that don't feel good.

Anxiety

Anxiety keeps Riley cautious and alert, helping her anticipate challenges but also making her overthink situations at times.

Embarrassment

Embarrassment occurs when Riley feels exposed or out of place. It helps her learn from awkward situations and navigate social norms.
