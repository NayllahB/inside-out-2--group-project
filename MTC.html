The Family

Meet Riley and her family members!

Mom

Riley's mom is a smart, kind woman who understands that teens and adults alike have complex emotions. She herself often feels confused how to be a good mother, and concerned about RIley's wellbeing.

Dad

Riley's dad is goofy guy who loves his family. He and Riley have a special relationship where they can have fun and play hockey. He sometimes has trouble knowing what his wife is thinking, but she loves him all the same.
