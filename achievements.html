Audience Review from Rotten Tomatoes

"Inside Out 2 is a gem that’s as funny and creative as it is heartwarming. Though it’s aimed at kids, this movie brilliantly captures human emotions in a way anyone can relate to. The storyline is easy to follow yet layered with meaning, making it educational for young viewers and deeply resonant for adults. Fear, in particular, really stood out to me. This character is portrayed so accurately that it makes you reflect on your own inner anxieties. Pixar has, once again, taken a complex topic and made it feel personal and approachable. This is more than a children’s film; it’s a reminder that every emotion has value, and understanding them can enrich us all."
