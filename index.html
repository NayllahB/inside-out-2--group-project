Welcome to the Mind of a Teenager

In "Inside Out 2," we revisit Riley as she navigates the rollercoaster of her teenage years. The familiar crew at Headquarters—Joy, Sadness, Anger, Fear, and Disgust—find themselves in for a surprise as new emotions make their way into Riley's life. When Anxiety arrives, it stirs up the dynamics and challenges the team's understanding of their roles. But she might just be the first of several unexpected visitors...
